Secure HTTP (HTTPS)

This data measures whether federal domains support the HTTPS protocol (https://), and the strength of that support. HTTPS provides a secure connection across the internet, and is becoming the new baseline for public web services. As part of this shift, the U.S. federal government is in the process of transitioning entirely to HTTPS.
